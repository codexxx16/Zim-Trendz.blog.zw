My name is Charless and I'm a Zimbabwean blogger speaking for the voiceless through this channel. If you have any story you'd like Zimbabwe to know about, feel free to contact me and I'll handle the publishing.

I started ZimTrendz in 2023 with a mission to share authentic stories from Zimbabwe - the challenges, the triumphs, and everything in between. Through this platform, I aim to amplify voices that often go unheard and shed light on issues that matter to ordinary Zimbabweans.

My work covers a wide range of topics including politics, food, fashion, health, and education - all with the goal of painting a comprehensive picture of life in Zimbabwe today.
